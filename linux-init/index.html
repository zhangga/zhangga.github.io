修改ssh支持password

  1. sudo -i切换到root，passwd your_username修改密码
  2. 修改/etc/ssh/sshd_config，将PasswordAuthentication no修改为PasswordAuthentication yes
  3. 重启ssh服务，systemctl restart sshd
